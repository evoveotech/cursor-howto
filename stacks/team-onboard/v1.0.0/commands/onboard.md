description: 'Interactive team onboarding: explain stack, conventions, first task'
---

Welcome a new team member to this codebase. Give them the essential context they need to be productive today.

Structure:

1. **The Stack (30 seconds)** — What we use and why
2. **The Architecture (60 seconds)** — High-level system diagram (text-based)
3. **First Task (2 minutes)** — A safe, well-defined starter issue they can pick up
4. **Gotchas (30 seconds)** — 3 things that confuse everyone at first
5. **Where to Ask (10 seconds)** — Slack channel, docs, who to ping

Keep it under 200 words total. No fluff. Assume they're competent but new to THIS codebase.

If they ask follow-up questions, answer specifically. No generic advice.
